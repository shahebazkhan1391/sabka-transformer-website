About Sabka Transformers

Sabka Transformers Pvt. Ltd. is a premier manufacturing unit dedicated to powering industries through reliable engineering. We are renowned for our heavy-duty transformer tanks, precision-engineered Core Coil Assemblies (CCA), and highly efficient Distribution Transformers.

Our commitment to quality has earned us the ISO 9001:2015 certification. Furthermore, as an MSEDCL Approved Vendor, we ensure strictly supervised manufacturing practices that align with state and national utility standards.
